Shift Partner

üìã Role & Scope

Lead UX Designer responsible for comprehensive heuristic evaluation, workflow redesign, and UI refinement of the NHS shift management platform used by clinical staff across multiple healthcare facilities.

üéØ The Problem

The existing shift management interfaces were creating significant friction for healthcare workers:

üõ† Design Actions

üìà Results & Impact
